College Student Compassion & Bias Data Analysis

Python, Pandas, Data Cleaning, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Data Visualization, Statistical Analysis

Description

Conducted an analysis of survey responses collected from other University of Maryland students to examine the relationship between various demographic factors and perceived "jerkiness" of strangers from Reddit's "r/AITA", a subreddit where users post stories and ask if they are the "a**hole" in a situation. Utilized statistical tests and visualizations in Python to explore the data and identify correlations.

Details

  • Performed extensive data cleaning including handling missing values, renaming columns, and merging datasets to prepare for analysis.
  • Created informative visualizations using seaborn and matplotlib to illustrate the distribution and relationships within the data.
  • Conducted a comprehensive analysis of survey data to identify trends and correlations between demographic factors and perceived "jerkiness".
  • Applied chi-square tests to determine the statistical significance of the relationships between categorical variables.
  • Discovered that students from a separate section of the course were not primed when providing their responses before continuing with the analysis.
  • Concluded that we must fail to reject the null hypothesis that there is no statistically significant difference between the responses to the scenarious about children between surveyees of different genders.
